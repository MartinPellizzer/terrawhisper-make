# 4. Microsemantic Tip (CRITICAL)

Your **first paragraph must define BOTH the symptom AND the solution context**:

Example:

> Headaches are a common pain symptom characterized by pressure, throbbing, or discomfort in the head or neck region, often caused by stress, dehydration, muscle tension, or underlying conditions. Herbal medicine is used to relieve headaches by reducing inflammation, relaxing muscles, improving circulation, and calming the nervous system through plant-based remedies such as peppermint, ginger, and feverfew.

This does 3 things:

1. Defines the **entity (headache)**
2. Introduces **causes**
3. Connects to **herbal medicine mechanisms**

→ This is **perfect Koray-style entity alignment**
